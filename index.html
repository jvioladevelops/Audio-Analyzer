ADSR Envelope Analyzer

The four stages include attack, decay, sustain, release. Attack is the initial run up time from 0 to peak amplitude. Decay is the time taken to reach sustain from the subsequent peak. Sustain is the amplitude level until the key is released, and lastly release is the time taken for the amplitude level to reach 0.

Amplitude Analyzer

This visualizer shows the amplitude of a song in real time. The amplitude of the sine wave determines the volume. The sliders correspond to different aspects of amplitude that you can adjust. Song: Skyrim-Dovakin Orchestra Version
